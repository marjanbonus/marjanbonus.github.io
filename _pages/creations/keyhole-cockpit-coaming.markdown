**Keyhole Cockpit Coaming**<br />
_Handcrafted steam-bent keyhole cockpit coaming with a three-strand black HT Polyester rope lip._<br />
-- Steam bent selected oak
-- Scarf joints at back are fastened with D4 polyurethane glue
-- Rope coaming edge/lip
-- Pre-drilled 3mm lacing holes<br />
**Coaming stock dimensions**
_Height: 3cm_
_Thickness: 1cm_
_Coaming rope lip thickness: 1.6cm_<br />
**Coaming inner dimensions:**
_Size: 41cm x 79.5cm_<br />
**Weight**
_Approx. 700g_<br />
**Finish**
_Not applied (covered with urethane during skin coating)_<br />
